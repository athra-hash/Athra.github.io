Background

The story
behind the work.

Growing up in Ampara, Sri Lanka, my path to the construction industry was anything but conventional. After navigating real financial hardship and working in vocational trades, I made a deliberate choice to invest in education — a decision that transformed my trajectory entirely.

"Distinction in every course I have taken. Batch topper three times over."

At BCAS Campus Kalmunai, I completed a Diploma in IT, a Draughtsman Diploma (CCDT), and a Diploma in English — achieving Distinction and batch topper status in all three. I am now completing my HND in Quantity Surveying under the Pearson program.

My goal is clear: to bring my technical expertise and relentless work ethic to the construction industry in the UAE or Qatar, and build a career defined by excellence.
